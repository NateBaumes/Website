TechTalks: The Great AI Debate

Debate the future of AI in this exciting competition!

📅 Date & Time: March 21, 2025 | 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

📍 Location: THURST 205

Debate Format

  • 📜 Participants will be given a list of AI-related topics to choose from a day before the event.
  • 🔄 Each participant will be randomly assigned to argue for or against a topic.
  • ⏳ 5 minutes to prepare your arguments.
  • 🎤 Each participant will have 2 minutes to present their arguments.
  • 🔄 After the presentation, each participant will be randomly assigned a rebuttal partner and will have 2 minutes to counter the opposing team’s arguments.

Evaluation Criteria

  • 🎯 Clarity of arguments
  • 🧠 Depth of knowledge
  • 🎙️ Persuasiveness of presentation

Guidelines

  • ⚖️ Respectful debate etiquette must be maintained at all times.
  • 🏆 Three winners will be selected for the competition.
