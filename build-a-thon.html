CodeCrafters: Innovate & Create Competition

Max 3 students per team; individual participation allowed

📅 Date & Time: March 21, 2025 | 2:15 PM - 4:15 PM

📍 Location: THURST 205

Competition Overview

In this exciting competition, teams (or individuals) will brainstorm and create innovative solutions to a problem statement given at the start of the event.

What You’ll Present

  • 🌐 A website presenting your idea and supporting information.
  • ⚡ A prototype (wireframe, design, or working model) demonstrating the concept.
  • 📢 A 5-10 minute presentation explaining your project and its potential impact.

Competition Guidelines

  • ⏳ Teams have 2 hours to complete their work.
  • 💻 External resources can be used, but plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  • 🏆 Two winners: Grand winner and Innovation Award.
  • 🏅 Honorable mentions for Best Website, Best Oral Presentation, and Best Prototype.
