AI Nexus: AI and the Future of Work

Faculty and Industry Panel Idea

Event Overview

AI Nexus: AI and the Future of Work will bring together a diverse panel of industry leaders, experts, and faculty members to discuss the role of Artificial Intelligence in shaping the future of the workplace.

Panel Discussion Topics

  • 🤖 The impact of AI on different industries
  • 💼 AI-driven innovations in the workplace
  • 📊 Ethical considerations in AI adoption
  • ⚙️ The future of work: human-AI collaboration

Panelists

Our panel includes distinguished faculty and industry experts from various fields who will share their insights and answer questions from the audience.

Guidelines

  • Arrive early to get a good seat for this highly anticipated discussion!
