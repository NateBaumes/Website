CodeCrafters Challenges

Instructions:

  • 1. Challenge Themes: There are five challenge themes available. Four themes are specific to the major categories: Computer Science, Cybersecurity, Data Science, and Business Analytics. One interdisciplinary theme is open to all majors.
  • 2. Theme Selection: You may choose only one theme to work on during the competition. You can choose a theme based on your expertise, interest, and team strengths. Once selected, your choice is final and cannot be changed after submission.
  • 3. Problem Statement: The detailed problem statement and deliverables for each theme will be provided at the event's start. Teams must develop their solution based on the problem statement and meet the specified deliverables.
  • 4. Pre-Event Preparation: You must not submit any work in advance. However, you may research and brainstorm potential solutions for your chosen theme. Teams are encouraged to familiarize themselves with the problem domain and relevant technologies.
